About Us

Our college is having Tecs,Robotics clubs etc.,But we felt like there is no club exclusively for Mathematics then came the idea of starting a club for maths,so during the month of February 2014 we started the MATHS CLUB at SASTRA with the permission of Dr.K.Kannan,The Dean of Humanities and Sciences and C.Natarajan who is the faculty of Humanities and Sciences department where both them have been behind us all the time and guided us well.Begining from its first day of initiation the club has been center for many activities.The following are the activities that are done by the club:

  • The official permission for the MATHS CLUB at SASTRA was given on February.
  • until the end of the month the recruitment process for the club was conducted and a core team was formed.
  • The lateral entry students were helped by conducting special classes the turn out was 60 students out of which 80% were satisfied
  • We conducted Mathematics Tresure Hunt in Anuvbhava 2014 in which 150 participants had participated
  • We helped second year students by placing soft copies of 14 previous year question paper solutions in our official website and its response came from 800 students
  • We initiated work on Mathematical Models

During semester holidays our team tuned for the following future plans:

  • Designed a website
  • Collected Aptitude Questions
  • Few of us worked on matlab for conducting workshop
  • Few wrote Magzines
  • Rest planned for the Events that are to be conducted during the semester
