About Us

The vision of MCS is quite simple. We want everyone to appreciate the joy of mathematics in all aspects of study and life.

Our club aims at bringing the essence of Mathematics to every student in the campus by organizing various events, workshops and aptitude exams. But how did this club come into existence? An exclusive club for Mathematics was started in the month of February, 2014 with the permission of Dr.K.Kannan, The Dean of Humanities and Sciences and C.Natarajan who is a faculty of Humanities and Sciences department at SASTRA, Thanjavur. Constant guidance and support from these esteemed professors as well as various other professors from various Departments from the University has lead to the club's success and has helped the club reach its pinnacle. Begining from its first day of initiation the club has been the center for many activities.

The following are the various agendas undertaken by the club:

  • The official permission for the MATHS CLUB at SASTRA was given in February,2014.
  • A month long recruitment process for the club was conducted and the core team was formed.
  • The club helps the Lateral entry applicants to catch up with their peers by conducting special classes and lectures.
  • We constantly conduct events to ensure that the spirit of mathematics is never lost inside the campus.
  • We help students by posting solutions to previous year question papers.
