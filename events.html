Treasure Hunt during Anubhava'14

This is a new venture taken up for the first time ever in SASTRA. A great enthusiasm was shown in various participants from all over SASTRA irrespective of gender, department and year. It turned to be one of the exiting events in Anubhava'14 with registrations around 800.

On the day of the event, there was hustle and bustle in the campus by the participants moving from block to block searching for clues. Finally, the jackpot is hit by a girl from ECE department who used all the clues and made the sentence "MATH IS FUN".
