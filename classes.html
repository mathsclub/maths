Regular Mathematics Classes

Maths club has been conducting classes for slow learners of Mathematics on weekends.This contains our semester Mathematics syllabus.These classes helped many students to score well in their semester exams.

Matlab Workshop

First Matlab workshop was conducted in Dec,14. Students showed immense interest in learning a new software and made it a huge sucess. Seeing the enthusiasm in students, we have begun to continue conducting workshop even in the following years.

Math related models

Few creative minds inside the club started working on models which would be soon displayed to the entire community of SASTRA.

KLF lectures

We often conduct Knowledge Learning Forum lectures with the speakers including various Mathematical experts. One was by Dr.A.K.Mallik from Department of Aerospace Engineering and Applied Mechanics, Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology in Feb,15 on "Magical Mathematics of Ramanujan".

Aptitude Classes

This is first club in SASTRA that took the challenge of solving Aptitude Questions and teaching classes.This is one area where even final year students face difficulty beacause one has to cross the round Aptitude Test to go to further rounds of selection process.So Maths club worked on this and decided to conduct classes on Aptitude solving.These classes take place on friday evenings
